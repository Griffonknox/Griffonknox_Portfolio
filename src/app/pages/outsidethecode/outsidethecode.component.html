Skateboarding

I’ve been skateboarding since I was about 8 years old, on and off throughout my life. Recently it’s become one of the most important ways I take care of my mental and physical health. Skateboarding challenges me to set goals, experiment, and stay persistent through trial and error — the same mindset I bring to my work as a developer.

Skating has always been more than just a hobby for me — it’s a way of thinking. Learning tricks requires breaking things down step by step, adapting when something doesn’t work, and celebrating small progress. It’s a constant reminder that growth comes from persistence and creativity.
